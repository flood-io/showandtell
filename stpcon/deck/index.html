Tuning Tips

  • Bracketing
  • Targeting
  • Moving (Bottlenecks)

"Halve and halve again"

Take a bracketing approach to problem solving. Instead of testing the whole system focus on a unit. You're solving a simulataneous equation, the less variables the easier it will be.

"Small targeted changes"

Since you can't predict what's going to happen you need great monitoring and strict change control.

"(re)Moving bottlnecks"

Difficult to know what the next bottleneck is before you remove the current bottleneck.
